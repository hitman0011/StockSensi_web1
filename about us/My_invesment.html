Key Metrics Explained

Current Price

•The latest trading price of the stock.

52-Week High

•The highest price the stock has reached in the last year.

52-Week Low

•The lowest price the stock has reached in the last year.

Dividend

•The annual payment made to shareholders, expressed as a dollar amount per share.

>Yield (%)

•The annual dividend expressed as a percentage of the current stock price, calculated as: [ \text{Yield} = \left( \frac{\text{Annual Dividend}}{\text{Current Price}} \right) \times 100 ]

Volume

•The number of shares traded during the most recent trading session.
